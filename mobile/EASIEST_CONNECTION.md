# ⚡ Easiest Way to Connect iPhone

**Step-by-step guide:**

---

## 🚀 **Step 1: Start Expo**

In your terminal (in `mobile` folder):
```bash
npm run start:lan
```

Wait for QR code to appear.

---

## 📱 **Step 2: Use iPhone Camera (Not Expo Go)**

1. **Open iPhone Camera app** (the regular camera, not Expo Go)
2. **Point camera at QR code** in your terminal/computer screen
3. **A notification will appear** at the top saying "Open in Expo Go"
4. **Tap the notification**
5. **Expo Go opens automatically** and loads your app!

---

## ✅ **That's It!**

**No need to:**
- ❌ Find "Enter URL manually" (doesn't exist in newer versions)
- ❌ Type URLs
- ❌ Use Expo Go's scanner

**Just use Camera app!** ✅

---

## 🔧 **If Camera Doesn't Detect QR Code**

### **Try:**
1. **Make QR code bigger:**
   - Zoom in terminal
   - Make terminal window larger
   - Move closer to screen

2. **Check Camera permissions:**
   - Settings → Camera → Make sure enabled

3. **Try Safari method:**
   - Open Safari on iPhone
   - Type: `exp://192.168.0.235:8081` (use your IP)
   - Safari will ask to open in Expo Go

---

## 🎯 **Most Reliable Method**

**Camera app → Scan QR → Tap notification → Done!**

**This works 99% of the time!** 📱✅
